Our Services

What Byan Research Provides?

Delve into the world of research with our qualitative and quantitative methodologies. From immersive focus group discussions and in-depth consumer interviews to online surveys and mystery visits, our diverse approaches provide unparalleled insights. Whether you seek deep understanding or data-driven precision, our research empowers informed decision-making.

  • Customer Understanding

    We are passionate about understanding your customers and market to help you unlock hidden opportunities and stay ahead of the competition. Our customer understanding services encompass Satisfaction Studies, where we delve deep into your customers' needs, desires, and pain points, and Importance Performance analysis, which prioritizes the aspects that matter most to your customers.

  • Market Scanning

    In the realm of Market Research, our expertise lies in Market Scanning for Prices, where we conduct thorough analyses to ensure you optimize your pricing strategy. Additionally, our Market Scanning for Competition and Client Brands enables you to grasp a comprehensive understanding of your competitive landscape and refine your brand positioning.

  • Marketing Study

    We use innovative approaches like Brand Equity Research to assess your brand's value in consumers' minds. Our Competitive Brand Study uncovers competitors' strengths and weaknesses. Mapping market opportunities identifies areas for growth. We explore Usage and Buying habits to align your offerings with consumer behavior. Testing Cycle and Concept Testing refine your products. TURF Analysis and Penalty Analysis optimize your product portfolio. Pricing Studies ensure your pricing resonates. Advertising Research types create a lasting impact. Segmented Generated and Snapshot of the Segment identify niche markets for tailored strategies.

  • Distribution Study

    Our Distribution Studies involve Geographic Mapping Surveys to optimize your distribution network, ensuring that your products reach the right customers at the right time. At Byan, our unrivaled expertise, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence make us your ideal partner in unraveling the intricacies of your market, powering your growth, and propelling your success
